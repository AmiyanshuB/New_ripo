Hostel

S.I.T. Group of Hostels runs 4 Boys Hostels and 2 Girls Hostels. Together can accomodate 2,100 students in a plinth area of 30,021 Sq.m. Each hostel block is managed under the supervision of a Deputy Warden who is a faculty member of S.I.T. He/She is assisted by managers and workers. Each room in all the hostels has been furnished with cots, tables, chairs, book racks and a ceiling fan. Parents and gaurdians of the students are being provided accomodation in guest house on nominal rent whenever they visit college and wish to stay during their visits. They are also permitted to use the mess facilities in the hostels.

Health Centre

Health Centre

    Health Centre is situated in our SIT Campus. It has started functioning from 5th October 1992.Health centre has two doctors and one staff nurse and male health worker and two supporting staff.Health centre has computerized patient data and drug store details.
    Dr. Sateesh Patil is working as Resident Medical officer since Sept 1992 in Siddaganga Institute of Technology Tumkur, which has a student strength of 4000 and staff strength of about 600. Dr. Lokesh Babu consultant Neuropsychiatrist will be available for consultation on every Wednesday in the evening hours(5.00PM to 6.00PM.)
    Resident medical Officer has been provided with quarters in the campus. He offers emergency services after the duty hours in the campus. About 1500 students reside in our campus Hostels and they receive the services from him.
    Health centre has 3 beds of day care facility and 3 beds of inpatient ward facility for the Staff members and Students of SIT.
    Daily out patient number of patients in the Health Centre is around 70 to 80 members. Daycare and admission of the patients depends upon the conditions. In case of emergency, we have the arrangement of shifting the patient to the higher centre for further treatment by our own ambulance.
    We have two ladies Hostels where in we have two lady doctors available for consultation in the evening hours for the students. Maintenance of ladies hostel centre is being done by SIT health Centre. RMO arranges Blood donation camps and Blood grouping camps and Vaccination camps in our campus.

Camps

    Free blood grouping camp for all first year students every year.
    Voluntary blood donation camp every year for all the students and staff member of SIT.
    Staff health checkup for all the staff members and their family members every 6 months.
    Vaccination camps for Hepeatitis B, Typhoid etc will be conducted every year.
    Health awareness programme will be organised for all the students & staff members.

Facilities

    Health Centre provides free out patient consultation to all the students and staff members.
    Outpatient Services includes free medical consultation, dressing of wounds, symptomatic injections, vaccination, vision testing, free symptomatic medicines.
    Daycare Services : Patient is admitted to day care ward, IV Drip services, Patient monitoring, Nebulisation are provided under medical supervision from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
    Ward : Patient is admitted to ward when there is no much medical supervision during night time. Patient is admitted in wards in case of Isolation infectious diseases, post operative followup patients, Jaundice followup and others.
    Referal Services : If there is medical emergency where there is a need of ICU care patient will be shifted by an ambulance to MOU Hospitals Tumkur Manipal Hospital or Vinayaka Hospital.
    Lab & Investigative Facilities : There is a ECG, Random Blood Sugar estimation available on payment basis.All basic lab investigations are done here with Tumkur Manipal Hospital on payment basis.
    Specialist Consultations : Neuro Psychiatrist on every Wednesday wiil be available for consultation for the students.
    Mediclaim Health Insurance Policy Services : Health Centre maintains the staff members details of health insurance floater mediclaim policy for permanent employees.

Library
    The library, centrally located in the campus, is housed in two independent 3 - storeyed buildings having a plinth area of 1851 Sq. Mts. The library building was built by a donation received from Sri T.N. Kempahonnaiah, a philanthropist from Tumkur. The library is having a collection of 1,38,083 volumes that includes 60,239 volumes under General, 35,311 volumes under the Book Bank, 14,683 volumes under SC/ST Book Bank and 27,850 other volumes. The collection includes reference works such as Dictionaries, Encyclopaedias, Hand Books and Standards.
    The library subscribed the e-resources mandated by AICTE i.e., 24252+ e-books from IEL Online, ELSEVIER (Engineering and Computer Science), Springer, Taylor & Francis, IEEE, Knimbus and Proquest. These e-resources can be accesses across the campus 24/7. The library has membership with reputed libraries in Bangalore, like British Library, NAL Library, IIMB Library and CMTI LIbrary by which our students and faculty can avail the reference services offered by the above Libraries.
    The seating capacity of the Library is 543 users at a time, which also includes two separate AC Reading Halls of a capacity of 150 users. The library is computerised with WEB - enabled open source software NewGenLib. The on-line public access catalog can be accessed across the campus through OPAC which facilitates to search the books by author, title.
